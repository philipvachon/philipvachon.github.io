About Me

My name is Philip. I want to live in a world with more artists who know how to communicate their vision. I'm helping to make that a reality by working in the music industry in marketing and by running PHILIP branding & design. I design logos, clarify brands, and code websites. I can also be found writing songs, drawing portraits, or reading about existentialism.

I am a graduate of the NYU Undergraduate Music Business Program and have years of experience in the music industry. I have interned at companies like Red Light Management, Glassnote Records, and Columbia Records. To get more information on my work experience, check out my resum√© below.
